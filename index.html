About us

U6 Consulting is a consulting group based in Perth, Western Australia. Founded in November 2018, U6 Consulting is comprised of six university students. We aim to bring a stronger, better organised and more unified student consulting services to Perth and Australian organisations. Allowing development to occur for both the student consulting team and the organisations we help.
